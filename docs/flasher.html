FoxFW v2.0 — Web Installer

Install FoxFW
on your Flipper Zero

This installer runs entirely in your browser — no software to install. Connect your Flipper via USB and choose the install method below.

⚡ Requirements: Chrome or Edge on desktop · Flipper Zero connected via USB · For the Standard Update, your Flipper must be on and showing the desktop. For DFU Recovery, hold the left and back buttons during boot until the screen goes dark.
Select install type
Standard Update: Your Flipper should be on and showing the desktop or any app screen. Plug in via USB. The firmware will be uploaded as a .tgz package to your SD card and installed on reboot.
DFU Recovery: Boot your Flipper into DFU mode by holding Left + Back while powering on until the screen goes dark and stays dark. Then connect via USB. The firmware (.dfu) is written directly to internal flash — your SD card files are untouched.
